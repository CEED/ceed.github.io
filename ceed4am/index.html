CEED Fourth Annual Meeting

August 11-12, 2020

Virtual Meeting on Zoom + Slack

Overview

CEED will hold its fourth annual meeting August 11-12, 2020 virtually using ECP Zoom for videoconferencing and Slack for small group discussions. The goal of the meeting is to report on the progress in the center, deepen existing and establish new connections with ECP hardware vendors, ECP software technologies projects and other collaborators, plan project activities and brainstorm/work as a group to make technical progress. In addition to gathering together many of the CEED researchers, the meeting will include representatives of the ECP management, hardware vendors, software technology and other interested projects.

Registration

If you plan to attend, please register no later than August 7th. There is no registration fee.

Meeting format

Based on your feedback during registration we can incorporate the following elements in the meeting:

  • Project review and updates from the CEED team
  • Contributed talks from AD, ST, vendors and external partners
  • Short pre-recorded talks/poster presentations
  • Technical discussions in small breakout groups
  • Software tutorials
  • Hackathon

Meeting Agenda

The meeting activities will tentatively take place 10:30am-6:30pm eastern time.

  • Live presentations will be on ECP Zoom, links to be posted the week of the meeting
  • We also have a Slack workspace for additional discussions

Questions?

For questions, please contact the meeting organizers at ceed-meeting@llnl.gov.
